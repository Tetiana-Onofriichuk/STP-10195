Mary’s Life: A Makeover Story

Rebuild Dreams. Redesign Life!

Start Decorating With Mary!

Game Description

In Mary’s Life, you follow the journey of a bold interior designer navigating heartbreak, career changes, and a cluttered mansion full of memories.

After losing her corporate job, Mary inherits a forgotten estate from a distant relative. But with crumbling walls and broken dreams, she must choose: sell it, or transform it into the design studio of her dreams.

With help from quirky neighbors, a mystery-loving grandma, and her mischievous cat Mochi, Mary embarks on a makeover journey that’s equal parts emotional and creative.

Match tiles, unlock décor, and reveal the stories behind each room she restores — one renovation at a time.

Key Features

  • Design-Driven Puzzles: Play through tile-matching levels to unlock furniture, décor, and tools for renovations.

  • Choices That Matter: Decisions influence décor paths, relationships, and story direction.

  • Heartfelt Storyline: Explore Mary's emotional highs and lows as she rebuilds her space and life.

  • Real-Life Inspiration: Discover real design trends, color theory tips, and décor trivia.

  • Custom Interior Projects: Choose paint colors, furniture styles, and floor plans in each room you renovate.

  • Free to Play: All players welcome — with optional boosts and visual upgrades.

  • Diverse Cast: Meet friendly neighbors, old flames, hidden rivals, and loyal sidekicks.

FAQ

Is the game focused on design?

Yes — puzzles unlock decorative options, and your choices influence room outcomes.

Can I personalize the design?

Absolutely! Choose styles, furniture, palettes, and layout paths.

Is it free to play?

Yes, with optional in-app purchases for boosters and visual themes.

Can I play offline?

Yes, most features work offline once downloaded.

Does the story have multiple endings?

Yes. Your design and dialogue choices impact relationships and the final renovation reveal.
